Bacon-Wrapped Stuffed Jalapenos

Jalapenos stuffed with surprises inside and wrappred altogether with bacon, a delicacy that will make your kids want more.

Ingredient

  • 24 medium jalapeno peppers
  • 1 pound uncooked chorizo or bulk spicy pork sausage
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 12 bacon strips, cut in half

Steps

  1. Make a lengthwise cut in each jalapeno, about 1/8 in. deep; remove seeds. Combine the sausage and cheese; stuff into jalapenos. Wrap each with a piece of bacon; secure with toothpicks.
  2. Grill, covered, over indirect medium heat for 35-40 minutes or until a thermometer reads 160Â°, turning once. Grill, covered, over direct heat for 1-2 minutes or until bacon is crisp.
