Hotdog Sliders with Mango-Pineapple Salsa

A meal that will definitely love by your kids. A hotdot sandwich made more delicious by mango-pineapple salsa.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cubed fresh pineapple (1/2 inch)
  • 1 cup cubed peeled mango (1/2 inch)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped sweet red pepper
  • 12 hotdogs
  • 12 hot dog buns, split
